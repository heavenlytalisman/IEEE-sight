About IEEE SIGHT

Mission

The Special Interest Group on Humanitarian Technology (SIGHT) program is a network of IEEE volunteers around the globe that partner with underserved communities and local organizations to leverage technology for sustainable development.

Vision

Underserved communities around the world are able to benefit from technology as they seek sustainable solutions to development challenges.

Values

  • Sustainable solutions for lasting impact
  • Local partnerships and community engagement
  • Continuous education and knowledge sharing
  • Technology for humanitarian purposes

SIGHT Groups Worldwide

Global Network of Volunteers

IEEE SIGHT groups are established worldwide, connecting volunteers with local communities to implement technology solutions for sustainable development.

Requirements for Active Groups:

  • Minimum 6 IEEE members to form a group
  • At least 2 activities per year
  • Regular elections and officer reporting
  • Partnership with local organizations

Founded in Kerala, India

IEEE SIGHT was established in 2011 in Kerala, India, and has since grown into a global network of humanitarian technology volunteers.
