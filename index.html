About IEEE SIGHT

The Special Interest Group on Humanitarian Technology connects IEEE volunteers globally[6]

Our Mission

IEEE SIGHT groups consist of at least six IEEE members who come together to learn about sustainable development, foster relationships with their local communities, and leverage technology to address key issues[6].

Objectives

  • Promote humanitarian technology activities and sustainable development[12]
  • Increase awareness of technology's potential to improve living standards[12]
  • Engage with NGOs, civil society organizations, and corporates[12]
  • Work with global engineering community bodies[12]
